A design pattern we used in our project is strategy. The strategy pattern is a behavioral software design pattern that enables selecting an algorithm at runtime. We used strategy to define a family of algorithms, put each of them into a separate class, and to make their objects interchangeable. We also used an iterator design pattern. Iterator is a behavioral design pattern that allows sequential traversal through a complex data structure without exposing its internal details.
